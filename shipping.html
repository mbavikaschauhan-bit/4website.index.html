Shipping Policy

Digital service delivery information

Last Updated: November 2025

Tradlyst is a digital platform that provides online trading journal and analytics services.

🚚

Digital Service Only

We do not ship or deliver any physical goods. All our services are accessible through our web platform at www.tradlyst.com and the Tradlyst web portal at app.tradlyst.com.

All services are delivered digitally — no physical shipping required.

⚡

Instant Access

Upon successful payment, users receive instant access to their chosen subscription plan or premium features.

There is no waiting period or manual activation required — access begins automatically after payment confirmation.

📧

Email Confirmation

Once your payment is complete, your subscription and account details are sent to your registered email address.

If you don't see the email within a few minutes, please check your spam or junk folder before contacting support.

🛠️

Support

If you do not gain access within 1 hour of completing your payment, please contact our support team at:

📧 contact@tradlyst.com

We'll verify your payment and ensure your account is activated promptly.

❌

No Shipping Policy

As all services are delivered digitally, there is no physical shipping, delivery tracking, or dispatch involved.

There are no shipping fees applicable to any Tradlyst product or service.
