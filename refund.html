Refund & Cancellation Policy

Effective Date: 1st November 2025
1

No Refund Policy

All payments made for Tradlyst subscriptions are final and non-refundable.

We do not offer any full or partial refund under any circumstance, including:

  • Unused subscription period
  • Accidental purchase
  • Non-usage of the platform
  • Dissatisfaction with features
  • Change of mind

Please review the plan details carefully before subscribing.

2

Cancellation Policy

You can cancel your subscription anytime via your account or by emailing us at contact@tradlyst.com.

  • Cancellation prevents future renewals.
  • Access remains active until the end of the current billing cycle.
  • No refund will be provided for the remaining period.
3

Exceptional Circumstances

Refunds may only be considered if:

  • A duplicate payment was made, or
  • A technical error caused a failed activation.

Refunds (if approved) will be processed within 7–10 business days via Razorpay to your original payment method.
