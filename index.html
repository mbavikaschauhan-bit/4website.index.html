How It Works

A simple, 6-step process to turn raw data into confident, data-driven decisions.

01

Collect Raw Trade Data

Quickly get your trading history into the system via CSV upload or detailed manual logging. Capture entries, exits, size, costs.

02

Structure & Enrich Information

Use Tradlyst's tagging system to add critical context – strategy used, emotions felt, mistakes identified, execution quality.

03

Visualize Performance Trends

Instantly see your progress through dynamic equity curves, P&L charts, win rate trackers, and performance breakdowns.

04

Leverage AI for Deeper Insights

Let the AI Analyst process your enriched data to automatically generate summaries, spot biases, and highlight patterns.

05

Make Data-Driven Adjustments

Use visualizations, reports, and AI feedback to confidently refine your strategies and make informed trading decisions.

06

Track Progress & Plan Ahead

Review your growth over time and plan smarter moves for the future. Identify trends, fine-tune strategies, and trade each session with renewed clarity and confidence.
