Manual Trade Entry

Log detailed information for each trade, including symbol, direction, segment, trading style, entry/exit dates & times, prices, quantities, stop loss, target, brokerage, and other charges.

Comprehensive Dashboard

Get an at-a-glance overview of your trading performance with Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) like Net P&L, Win Rate, Profit Factor, and Average Win/Loss. Includes Account Summary and Open Positions Overview.

Psychology Tracking

Tag trades with your emotional state (Calm, Anxious, Confident, etc.) and identify common mistakes (Overtrading, Ignored Stop Loss, etc.) to understand behavioral patterns.

Strategy Tagging

Assign predefined or custom strategy tags to your trades for performance analysis by strategy.

Trading Calendar View

Visualize your trading days on a calendar, showing daily P&L and allowing quick access to trades made on a specific day.

Detailed Trade History

View, filter, and manage all your past trades in a comprehensive log with options to edit or delete entries. Includes bulk delete functionality.

Fund Management Ledger

Track your deposits and withdrawals to maintain an accurate account balance history.

Advanced Reporting Suite

Deep dive into your performance with various charts and metrics, including Account Balance Curve, Equity Curve, Daily & Monthly P&L Bar Charts, P&L by Trading Style & Segment, Win/Loss Distribution, Performance by Day of the Week, Net P&L by Mood, Most Common Mistakes Analysis, and Detailed Performance Metrics Table.

Detailed Trade Statement

Generate a filterable statement of all transactions (trades, deposits, withdrawals) with an option to export as CSV.

CSV Bulk Trade Upload

Import multiple trades at once using a formatted CSV file.

Capital Growth Challenge

Set and track progress towards specific capital growth goals within a defined timeframe. Includes challenge history.

AI Analyst (Initial Version)

Get AI-powered insights, alerts on potential issues (like overtrading or risk management), trader personality profiling, behavioral analysis, and actionable recommendations based on your data.

User Profile Management

Update your personal information and manage your account password.

Switch between light and dark themes for comfortable viewing.
